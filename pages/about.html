Eco Solutions for life...

Ustawi Eco Solutions Limited is a pioneering company specializing in sustainable waste management through innovative and eco-friendly practices. Our core focus revolves around harnessing the potential of black soldier fly larvae to efficiently decompose organic waste and transform it into valuable organic feeds for poultry, fish, and pigs, as well as high-quality organic manure.

Our Mission

Ustawi Eco Solutions Limited is dedicated to revolutionizing waste management by transforming organic waste into valuable resources, promoting sustainable agriculture, and contributing to a healthier and more environmentally conscious society. By choosing Ustawi Eco Solutions, businesses and communities not only address their waste management challenges but also actively participate in building a more sustainable and resilient future.
