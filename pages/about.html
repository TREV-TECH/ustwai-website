About Us

Home / About Us
Eco Solutions for life...

Ustawi Eco Solutions Limited is a pioneering company specializing in sustainable waste management through innovative and eco-friendly practices. Our core focus revolves around harnessing the potential of black soldier fly larvae to efficiently decompose organic waste and transform it into valuable organic feeds for poultry, fish, and pigs, as well as high-quality organic manure.

Our Goal

At Ustawi Organic Farms, community involvement is a cornerstone of our mission. We actively engage with local and global communities to foster sustainable practices, educate on the benefits of Black Soldier Fly larvae technology, and support food security initiatives. Through partnerships with schools, nonprofits, and local businesses, we facilitate workshops and seminars aimed at spreading knowledge about sustainable agriculture and waste management. Our volunteer programs encourage hands-on participation in creating greener, healthier environments, empowering individuals to make impactful changes.

Our Mission

Our mission is to revolutionize the way the world views and utilizes natural resources, by leading the forefront in sustainable and innovative solutions. We are dedicated to harnessing the potential of Black Soldier Fly larvae (BSFL) technology to address critical environmental challenges, improve food security, and create value from organic waste. Through the production of high-quality protein, organic frass, and BSFL oil, we aim to provide eco-friendly alternatives that reduce the environmental footprint of traditional agriculture and industry.

Our Vision

Our vision is a world where sustainability and innovation intersect to create a harmonious relationship between humanity and the planet. We strive to be at the vanguard of transforming waste into wealth, utilizing Black Soldier Fly larvae (BSFL) technology to pioneer a future where food production and waste management systems are sustainable, resilient, and circular by design. We envision a global community that embraces alternative proteins and sustainable agricultural practices.
